ID Card Application

Processing: 1-3 business days Category: Identity Services Most Requested

Service Description

Apply for a government-issued ID card used for identification, transactions, and access to public services within Atlantis City. The card includes a photo, full name, birth date, and a unique ID number.

This service is open to all residents 15 years and older. Minors must be accompanied by a guardian during application.

Requirements

  • Completed ID Card Application Form
  • Original and photocopy of Birth Certificate
  • One (1) government-issued valid ID (if available)
  • Proof of Residency (e.g., utility bill, barangay certificate)
  • Recent 1x1 or 2x2 photo (white background)
  • Payment of application fee

Reminder

Photos can be taken onsite free of charge. Applicants must wear appropriate attire.

Application Process

1. Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents and ensure photocopies are clear and legible.

2. Submit Application

Visit your nearest ID Application Center or apply online via ServeEase Portal.

3. Photo Capture & Biometrics

Have your photo taken and fingerprint scan recorded for system identification.

4. Verification

All submitted documents are verified by municipal ID processing staff.

5. Claim ID Card

Receive notification once your ID card is ready. Claim it in person or request delivery.

Fees & Payments

  • Standard ID Application: PHP 150.00
  • Replacement for Lost ID: PHP 200.00
  • Damaged Card Replacement: PHP 100.00

Fees can be paid via the ServeEase portal or at the city treasurer's cashier. Discounts apply for senior citizens and PWDs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I lose my ID card?

You must report it immediately. You’ll need to file an affidavit of loss and pay the replacement fee to reissue a new card.

Can I update my ID if my address or name changes?

Yes. Submit the change request form along with valid proof (e.g., marriage certificate, barangay certificate) and your existing ID card.

How long does it take to get my ID?

Processing usually takes 1-3 business days. Some locations offer express same-day issuance for urgent requests.
