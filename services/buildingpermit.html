Building Permit Application

Processing: 5-7 business days Category: Construction Popular Service

Service Description

This permit is required for all new construction, renovations, additions, and demolition projects within Atlantis City. The permit ensures that all construction meets local building codes, zoning regulations, and safety standards.

Applications are reviewed by our Building Safety Department to verify compliance with the International Building Code (IBC) and local ordinances. Projects over $50,000 valuation require additional plan reviews.

Requirements

  • Completed application form (signed by property owner or authorized agent)
  • Two sets of construction drawings (to scale) including site plan, floor plans, and elevations
  • Proof of property ownership or notarized authorization from owner
  • Contractor information and license verification
  • Energy compliance documentation (for new construction)
  • Payment of applicable fees (varies by project valuation)

Important Note

Residential projects under 200 sq.ft. may qualify for an express permit process with reduced requirements. Commercial projects always require full documentation.

Application Process

1. Pre-Application

Consult with our planning department to verify zoning and special requirements

2. Submit Application

Complete application with all required documents through our portal or in-person

3. Plan Review

Our team reviews submitted plans for code compliance (5-7 business days)

4. Approval & Payment

Pay fees and receive your approved permit to begin construction

5. Inspections

Schedule required inspections at key construction milestones

6. Final Approval

Receive certificate of occupancy upon successful final inspection

Fees & Payments

Permit fees are calculated based on project valuation:

  • First PHP 20000: PHP 1000 base fee
  • PHP40000 PHP1000 + per additional PHP20000
  • $50,001+: $785 + $10 per additional $1,000 over $50,000

Additional fees may apply for plan review, inspections, and special assessments. Payment can be made online or at the Building Department office.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I need to make changes after permit approval?

Minor changes may be submitted as an amendment to your existing permit. Significant changes may require a new permit application. Contact the Building Department for guidance on your specific changes.

How long is my building permit valid?

Permits are valid for 180 days from issuance. If work hasn't begun within this period, you'll need to apply for an extension. Once work begins, the permit remains valid as long as work progresses continuously.

Can I do the work myself or do I need a licensed contractor?

Homeowners may perform work on their own primary residence, except for electrical, plumbing and HVAC work which always requires licensed contractors. All commercial work requires licensed professionals.
