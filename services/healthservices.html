Health Services Application

City Health Office Processing Time: 2–3 Days

Requirements

  • Completed Health Services Application Form
  • Valid Government-issued ID
  • Proof of Residence (Barangay Certificate)
  • Medical History Record (if applicable)

Application Process

1. Submit Application

Fill out the form and upload necessary documents via the portal.

2. Initial Review

The City Health Office will evaluate your request and contact you for any clarification.

3. Health Assessment

Schedule and attend your in-person or telehealth consultation (if required).

4. Approval and Notification

You’ll receive an email or SMS once your request is approved.

FAQs

Do I need a Barangay Certificate?
Yes, it confirms your local residency, which is required to access government health services.
Can I request a specific physician?
We try to accommodate requests depending on availability.
Is this service free?
Yes, for residents. Charges may apply for specialized services.
