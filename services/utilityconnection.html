Utility Connection Application

Processing Time: 3-5 business days Office: Municipal Utility Office

Overview

Apply for a new utility connection for electricity, water, or other municipal services. This service allows residents and businesses to request installation or transfer of utility connections efficiently.

Requirements

  • Completed application form (provided below)
  • Valid government-issued ID (e.g., Driver’s License, Passport)
  • Proof of property ownership or tenancy agreement
  • Recent utility bill (if transferring service)
  • Payment of processing fee (if applicable)
  • Additional documents as required by the Municipal Utility Office

Application Process

Step 1: Download and Fill Application Form

Download the application form below, complete it accurately with your details.

Step 2: Submit Requirements

Submit the filled form along with required documents at the Municipal Utility Office or via the online portal.

Step 3: Payment

Pay the processing fee as instructed. Payment methods include cash, online transfer, or mobile payment apps.

Step 4: Inspection and Installation

Utility personnel will inspect your premises and schedule installation or connection setup.

Step 5: Confirmation

Once installed, you will receive a confirmation and billing details.
