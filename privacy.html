Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use ServeEase, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address.
  • Transaction Data: Payment details (processed securely via third-party providers).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Process service requests and payments.
  • Improve our platform and user experience.
  • Send important updates (e.g., request status changes).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Security

We implement:

  • SSL encryption for all data transfers.
  • Regular security audits.
  • Strict access controls to personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

We partner with trusted providers for:

  • Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal).
  • Analytics (Google Analytics).

These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account (contact support).

6. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of ServeEase constitutes acceptance.
